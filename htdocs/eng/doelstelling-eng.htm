Objectives:

 

  • To set-up and maintain a continuous registration of data relating to all children admitted to all pediatric intensive care departments in the Netherlands.

 

  • Giving insight in kind and severity of their illness, treatment and outcome.

 

  • The comparison of the different departments (benchmarking) on structure, process and outcome, which can lead to adjustments and improvements in the organization and standards of care.

 

  • To follow and evaluate changes in policies on structure an process
    such as harmonization with the Neonatology departments, developments in capacity, setting up national and regional transport systems.

 

  • Contributing to the development of quality indicators for the pediatric intensive care.
